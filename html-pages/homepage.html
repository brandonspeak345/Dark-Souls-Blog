Introduction


Welcome to my web page about the video game series "Dark souls", and other games made by the company "FromSoftware". In this blog, I am going to describe each game from the company in detail, and list what I both like and dislike, starting with the original "Dark Souls" before working my way through in chronological order.
At the top of the page, there is a drop down menu; this menu has links to the wikipedia pages of every game listed. Alternatively, if you would like more specific information about an individual game, click here for a link to the fextra-life wiki, which has a lot of very specific information about all of the "FromSoftware" games, as well as a multitude of other games.
There are also links to a number of my different socials, incase you would like to contact me with questions, feedback or would like me to elaborate on any comments made. These social links include; Facebook, Instagram, X, and my email address.

About Me

My name is Brandon Speak, and I am from the United Kingdom. For years, I have been a massive fan of these games, and I have fallen in love with every aspect of them, the longer I've played.
My first interaction with these games would be when I was around 14 years of age, and I first picked up a copy of Dark Souls 1 from my local game shop for cheap. I had heard great things about this game, and also understood how notoriously difficult this game was. Still, this did not deter me, and I proceeded to spend hours upon hours with this game, learning it's complex mechanics and even it's basic ones. I had never played anything as intricate and truly difficult as this, but that's what made me love it even more. Once I bested this game, there was no stopping me, and my addiction towards these games and their brutal difficulty only grew stronger.
This website is my very first foray into the world of web development and coding. This is absolutely the first time i've used any code at all, and it has been built entirely out of Html, CSS, and Javascript. I've done my best to make it as polished and bug-free as I could, but seeings as this is my first try, there might be some things that slip through the cracks. Any feedback towards this page or any other pages linked to this site would be great, as I'm determined to grow.
